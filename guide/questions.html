Forming Questions

Yes/No

Add lhan at the start of a declarative sentence.

  • lhan wa fa fethr?
    English: Do you speak?

Information (wh- style)

Combine a case prefix with lhan to ask for a specific role:

FormMeaning
si-lhanhow
ti-lhanwhere
to-lhanwhen
ya-lhanto whom / what

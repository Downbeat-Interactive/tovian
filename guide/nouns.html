Definiteness

Speakers usually rely on context for definiteness, but optional prefixes can clarify:

SubjectNon-subject
Definitei-a-
Indefinitee-o-

Examples (definiteness)

  • i-la-driel fa mril a-ti-lo-shithil — “The person sees in the city.”
  • e-la-driel fa mril o-ti-lo-shithil — “A person sees in a city.”

Examples

  • yu-la-driel fa mril — English: [He/She] sees the person.
  • ti-lo-shithil la-driel fa mril — English: The person sees in the city.
  • hi-la-driel — English: of the person.

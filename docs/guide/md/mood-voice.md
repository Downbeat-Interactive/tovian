# Mood & Voice

Mood prefixes attach to the auxiliary:
- `wi-` subjunctive
- `se-` imperative
- `ko-` conditional
- `xo-` counterfactual
- `hle-` optative
- `tlo-` obligative
- `ef-` necessative
- `sho-` potential

Voice prefixes:
- `te-` reflexive
- `pa-` passive
- `mo-` middle
- `ke-` causative
- `ra-` reciprocal
- Unmarked: active

Mood + voice can combine: mood prefix before voice prefix on the auxiliary.
